The Surf Log is for people who still love clean little walls. It is not trying to rank every break on earth, chase live conditions, or turn surfing into another noisy dashboard.

V1 is deliberately simple: browse the list, mark waves you want to hit, mark the ones you have hit, and keep a quiet record in your browser.
